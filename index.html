Overview

devjcat LLC is a boutique B2B technology consultancy specializing in custom software engineering, artificial intelligence integration, and advanced IT solutions. We partner with global businesses to build scalable, high-performance applications and automate complex technical workflows.

With over 15 years of commercial engineering experience, our core expertise lies in backend architecture, modern full-stack development, and cutting-edge AI systems (including LLM Agents, LangGraph, and Model Context Protocol). From refactoring legacy code to designing sophisticated multi-agent infrastructures, we deliver clean, maintainable, and efficient solutions tailored to our clients' unique business needs.

Operating entirely remotely, we provide dedicated B2B contracting and consulting services to enterprise clients across the United Kingdom, Europe, and worldwide.
